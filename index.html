Introduction

Hi! My name is Harry Potter. I am currently in my 6th year at Hogwarts School for Witchcraft and Wizardry. Since I learned that I was a wizard, I have dreamed of becoming an auror. However, the last couple of years have done anything but help me reach that goal. Two years ago, I witnessed the return of Lord Vordemort and the death of my friend, Cedric Diggery. Since that time, I have felt a burden to fight against Vordemort, as I am the only one who can actually beat him. At least, I am the only person who has beat him before. This website will help you better understand me.
